Vacancy: Junior Product Owner

I’m looking for you!

Have you just graduated with your Bachelor’s or Master’s and are considering to start your career in tech as a Product Owner?

Nice-to-have keywords

  • Enthusiastic, easy learner, team player, flexible, pragmatic, communicative.

About the role

  • Work together, side-by-side with a senior Product Owner.
  • Together you’ll work on real tech assignments at reputed companies. Every workday an hour or two is reserved for individual training, questions, how-to’s, reviewing your work.
  • Assignments are usually either web focused or app focused (or both).
  • The aim is to get you up and running asap, kickstart you into tech, and launch your career.

You’ll learn on the job

  • How to prioritize your sprint
  • Technical skills: Jira, Google Analytics, Firebase (depends on assignment)
  • Scrum ceremonies
  • How to manage stakeholders and team mates
  • How to solve problems, analyze data and how to write (e.g. technical docs)
  • Performing customer research / UX testing (depends on assignment)

Hours & Pay

The amount of hours is flexible. You’ll be paid as a contractor working for ProductOwner.com.au, according to your worked hours.

Interested? Want to apply, or have some questions? Please reach out via the form.

Please note: To be eligible to apply, you must have Australian citizenship or permanent residency status. Unsolicited CVs from agencies will not be accepted.
