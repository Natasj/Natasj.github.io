Vacancy: Senior Product Owner

I’m looking for you!

Do you have 5-7 years of experience as a Product Owner, Digital Product Manager, Digital Business Analyst, or Digital Project Manager?

Nice-to-have keywords

  • Enthusiastic, easy learner, team player, flexible, pragmatic, communicative.

About the role

  • Varies per assignment
  • Assignments are usually either web focused or app focused (or both).

You have experience

  • Leading a multi functional team (Front-End, Back-End, Android, iOS)
  • Prioritizing your sprint
  • Technical skills: Jira, Google Analytics, Firebase, manual testing
  • Soft skills: How to manage stakeholders and team mates
  • Managing all Scrum ceremonies
  • How to solve problems, analyze data and how to write (e.g. technical docs/jiras)

Hours & Pay

The amount of hours is flexible. You’ll be paid as a contractor working for ProductOwner.com.au, according to your worked hours.

Interested? Want to apply, or have some questions? Please reach out via the form.
