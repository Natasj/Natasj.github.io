About ProductOwner.com.au

ProductOwner.com.au is a boutique product agency, specialised in optimising B2C product roll-out for Web and Apps, recruitment and training.

We help companies grow and create top-notch tech products together by filling interim positions:

  • Product Owner
  • Product Owner/Scrum Master combination
  • Scrum Consultant
  • Product Consultant
  • Head of Product

In addition, we provide training on the job:

Launching a new agency concept: Train & Transfer - No more gaps after your contractor has left

Train

Pairs - We send 1 senior Product Owner together with 1 junior Product Owner to fulfil the client's assignment or solve client's problem. Both Product Owners (junior and senior) will blend into client's development team together.

The junior Product Owner

  • will gradually learn all Product Owner skills from the senior Product Owner
  • will be fully onboarded at the client: knowing the stakeholders, the colleagues, and internal processes
  • will be fully prepared for BAU: How to prioritize, how to lead a team of developers, understand the analytics and metrics, Jira, optional: Lead scrum ceremonies

ProductOwner.com.au charges a daily rate for the Product Owner pair. The junior is paid by ProductOwner.com.au.

Eventually, the junior is fully trained, operational, and can immediatly take over the BAU tasks of the senior Product Owner.

Transfer

After the assignment has been completed, the senior Product Owner goes to the next assignment, and there is the opportunity for the client to hire the trained and onboarded junior Product Owner. (This is optional for the client, and in mutual agreement with the junior).

The transfer can be into a permanent position, or into a contracting extension via ProductOwner.com.au.

Benefits

In comparison to hiring a contractor, a trained and onboarded junior

  • is more cost-effective
  • will most likely stay around for a longer time
  • will become part of your team and will adopt your company culture

Duration

Depending on what kind of services you need, our contracts can be hourly, per day, short-term (1-3 months), mid-term (6 months), or longer term (1-2 years).
