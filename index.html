    About ProductOwner.com.au

    What does ProductOwner.com.au do?
    What has ProductOwner.com.au has to offer the market?

    ProductOwner.com.au is a boutique product agency, specialized in maturing and optimizing product roll-out, influenced by the European way of working. We help companies grow and create and deliver top-notch tech products together. We offer
  • Execution of current product and scrum practises
  • Product consulting
  • Scrum/agile/change consulting
  • Feel free to reach out to discuss any other kind of consulting or assignments we could help you with
    • Our contracts can be short-term (1-3 months), mid-term (6 months), or longer term (1-2 years).

    For clients: What do I get when I hire ProductOwner.com.au?

    Teamwork! Aimed for is to send a team of 1 senior Product Owner acting, together with 1 or 2 junior Product Owners (in training) who will assist the senior, and will gradually learn the skills over time from the senior. After completion: Possibility to hire a fully trained Product Owner junior After the assignment has been completed, there will be the opportunity to hire the junior Product Owner who by then has already been trained on the job within your company (via ProductOwner.com.au/perm; in mutual agreement). Roadmap In 2021, we want to start fully operating but we’re still in our beta phase (= we’re experimenting and testing the objective of ProductOwner.com.au). Nat is the senior. Junior hiring is currently in progress. Want to work for us? In 2022, it potentially will be an option to hire Nat as an interim Head of Product or Product Owner Consultant to train/share knowledge with your current set of product owners/managers.

    Vacancy: Junior Product Owner at ProductOwner.com.au

    I’m looking for you!

    Have you just graduated with your Bachelor’s or Master’s and are considering to start your career in tech as a Product Owner? Nice-to-have keywords: Enthusiastic, easy learner, team player, open-minded, flexible, self-starter, creative mind, pragmatic, problem solver, communicative, and you don’t give up easily. (Don’t worry if you don’t think you match on all. It’s all about having the potential to learn and grow)

    About the role

  • Work together, side-by-side with a senior Product Owner.
  • Together you’ll work on real tech assignments at reputed companies. Every workday an hour or two is reserved for individual training, questions, how-to’s, reviewing your work.
  • Assignments are usually either web focused or app focused (or both).
  • The aim is to get you up and running asap, kickstart you into tech, and launch your career.
    • You’ll learn everything on the job: Prioritizing, stakeholder management,analytical skills (Google analytics/Firebase), writing skills, document drafting, scrum ceremonies (refinement, standup, sprint review, definition of done/ready etc), soft skills, talking with designers and developers, jira, technical skills, testing, technical writing, roadmapping and depending on the client: maybe customer research / UX testing. This is a paid role, no internship. The amount of hours is flexible. Forty would be great as you’d learn fast. However, we’re flexible to accommodate. You’ll be paid as a contractor working for ProductOwner.com.au, according to your worked hours. Interested? Want to have more information or have some questions? Please send Nat a message via the form. Please note: To be eligible to apply, you must have Australian citizenship or permanent residency status. Unsolicited CVs from agencies will not be accepted.

    Mission

    Share and apply European product knowledge, processes, and mindset. Help companies grow and create and deliver top-notch tech products together. Kickstart careers of Australia's indiginous fresh grads by introducing them into Sydney's tech society, creating tech opportunities and equality.

    Our values are

    Equality - We’re all equal and deserve the same chances and opportunities. Deliver excellent quality - We always try our best, work hard, and deliver top-notch quality. We don’t disappoint. Together - We do everything together. Everyone is across our plans. Stakeholders are informed and agree with our approach. Always feel free to ask me anything. We’re in this together, no stupid questions. We’re agile! - Be creative but ‘fail fast’ by testing assumptions early in the process without investing too much time or effort. Have an idea, ask your colleague for their opinion or try it in a small setup. Step by step - If we can’t solve a problem today, we try it again tomorrow. Never give up. We are resilient. We’re accountable. When we mess up, we talk about it and learn from it. Simplicity - We explain difficult methods/theories/ways of working in an easy comprehensible way for everyone. We are natural translators of technical problems. (No worries, I can teach you!) Work hard, play hard - This one is cheesy, I know, but I want to make sure it’s clear that it’s not all tough work. Tech is FUN. We’ll have a blast.
