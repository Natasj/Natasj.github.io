About ProductOwner.com.au

ProductOwner.com.au is a boutique product agency, specialized in maturing and optimizing product roll-out, influenced by the European way of working.

We help companies grow and create and deliver top-notch tech products together.

Specialization
  • We can fill interim positions e.g. Product Owner, Scrum/Product Consultants, and Head of Product
  • B2C (Business-to-Consumer): Consumer-facing apps and websites
  • Web: Front-end, Back-end. Apps: Android, iOS

We can help you with

BAU
  • Delivering a project, a new product or features
  • Leading a development team
  • Maintaining an existing product
  • Optimize existing product or flows (identify bottlenecks with analytics/user feedback, UX research, A/B testing)
  • Create roadmaps
  • Identify, set and monitor business or product KPIs

Scrum & Agile
  • Facilitate scrum meetings (stand-up, refinement, planning, sprint demo, retrospective, Definition of Ready, etc)
  • Prioritization, and managing sprints
  • Write stories, explain/present requirements to developers

Stakeholder management
  • Collect requirements and gather risks from the developers and stakeholders
  • Communicate plans and progress of development with stakeholders

Consulting
  • Improve internal business processes
  • Improve agile work processes within development teams
  • Product consulting

Feel free to reach out to discuss any other kind of consulting or assignments we could help you with

Our contracts can be short-term (1-3 months), mid-term (6 months), or longer term (1-2 years).

Why ProductOwner.com.au?

Last year I moved to Sydney. By working in Australia, I noticed my European manner of product owning is very much appreciated. I founded ProductOwner.com.au, because I want to share my knowledge and my way of working with others: other businesses, but also other (upcoming) product owners.

What do we offer?

Teamwork!

We send you a team of 1 senior Product Owner, together with 1 or 2 junior Product Owners (in training) who will assist, and will gradually learn the skills over time from the senior.

Take over

After the assignment has been completed, there will be the opportunity to hire the junior Product Owner who by then has already been trained on the job within your company (via ProductOwner.com.au/perm; in mutual agreement).

Our Roadmap

  • 2021: Launch. Current status: Looking for a suitable assignment, and looking for 1 or 2 Junior Product Owners.
