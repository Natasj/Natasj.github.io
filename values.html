Our values are

  • Equality - We’re all equal and deserve the same chances and opportunities.

  • Deliver excellent quality - We always try our best, work hard, and deliver top-notch quality. We don’t disappoint.

  • Together - We do everything together. Everyone is across our plans. Stakeholders are informed and agree with our approach. Always feel free to ask me anything. We’re in this together, no stupid questions.

  • We’re agile! - Be creative but ‘fail fast’ by testing assumptions early in the process without investing too much time or effort. Have an idea, ask your colleague for their opinion or try it in a small setup.

  • Step by step - If we can’t solve a problem today, we try it again tomorrow. Never give up. We are resilient. We’re accountable. When we mess up, we talk about it and learn from it.

  • Simplicity - We explain difficult methods/theories/ways of working in an easy comprehensible way for everyone. We are natural translators of technical problems. (No worries, I can teach you!)

  • Work hard, play hard - This one is cheesy, I know, but I want to make sure it’s clear that it’s not all just tough work. Tech is FUN. We’ll have a blast.
